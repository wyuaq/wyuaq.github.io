About me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at UBC, working with Prof. Vincent Wong. I received my Ph.D. in Electronic and Computer Engineering at HKUST in 2025, supervised by Prof. Khaled B. Letaief. Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Science and Engineering from Nanjing University in 2021, advised by Prof. Shaowei Wang. From Sept. 2024 to Jul. 2025, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at MIT, where I worked with Prof. Lizhong Zheng.

My research explores the intersection of signal processing and machine learning to develop advanced algorithms for next-generation wireless communications and sensing. I am interested in application domains such as millimeter-wave and terahertz systems, near-field communications and sensing, and ultra-massive MIMO systems. I received the National Scholarship in 2018 and the Hong Kong Ph.D. Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) in 2021.
